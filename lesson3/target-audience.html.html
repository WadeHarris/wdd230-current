The Target Audience

The target audience would be small to medium sized business owners in the Athlone area. Working class community members are also part of the target audience. By reaching out to this audience, they will have the opportunity to join the Athlone Chamber of Commerce and enjoy the benefits to the local businesses and community.

Personas

One persona would be a small to medium sized tyre shop owner looking to increase his customer base and promote his business a bit more within the community.

Another persona would be a community member looking for convenient service and to support local businesses. For example a father could be looking for a nice restaurant to take his family to but he might not know of any in the area.

Scenarios

How can I promote my business in my community?

Where can I buy new tyres in my area?

Where are the local restuarants in my area?

Which events can I attend to gain exposure for my business?

How can I connect with other businesses in the community?
