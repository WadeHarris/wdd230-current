Athlone Soccer Stadium

Athlone Soccer Stadium is almost like a landmark for the cmmunity of Athlone. Many people in the town often spend their Saturday agfternoons at the stadium either watching a sporting event or supporting a local market.

Nantes Park

Nantes Park is where you will find most of the town's youth and children. Its the most popular park in town and a crowd favorite when it comes to families meeting up and spending some time together having a picnic or some fun in the sun.

Athlone Swimming Pool

Athlone Swimming Pool has been around since the 1990's and it's still a crowd favorite on the hot days during summer.

Wembley Road House

Wembley Road House is by far the most famous spot to grab a bite of delicious food. All of Athlone go to Wembley to enjoy their tasty burgers and various meals on offer.

Athlone Library

The Athlone Library has been around for over decade and is often vistied by the elderly and primary schools in the town. Almost everyone in Athlone has memories of going to the Athlone Library and most people continue to take their children for a good read every now and then.
